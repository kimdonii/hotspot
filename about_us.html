About Us

We are a group of three girls who met each other at the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at JP Morgan Chase. We made this website for teens as a final project to end our summer on a high note! We wanted to inform teens about places and events to go to in the Brooklyn/Manhattan general area. We targeted teens because of the difficulty that comes with searching for safe, friendly, entertaining places appropriate for the age group. So we decided to single out all the places that fit this criteria so that people like us could hangout in style! This website is sectioned off into categories, so users can particularly locate the places that satisfy their current whim. Read more about each of the team members below!
